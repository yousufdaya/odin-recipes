Cheese Quesadilla

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for a snack that will have your mouth watering!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Steak (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan until warm.
  2. Place a tortilla in the pan.
  3. Place cheese and optional steak on one half of the tortilla.
  4. Fold the tortilla. Flip when the cheese starts to melt.
  5. Enjoy!
