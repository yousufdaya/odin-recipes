Tuna Sandwich

Description

Need protein? A good tuna sandwich will never disappoint.

Ingredients

  • Can of Tuna
  • Bread
  • Spices
  • Mayo
  • Hot Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast bread. Set aside.
  2. Open the can of tuna. Drain Water. Set aside in large bowl.
  3. Combine spices, mayo, and optional hot sauce with tuna.
  4. Spread on bread.
  5. Enjoy!
